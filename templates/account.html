Privacy Notice

What data we collect

  • Your username and a hashed version of your password (we never store your actual password)
  • Journal entries you write, along with the emotions and behaviour states our ML model detects
  • Your intentions (goals), identity beliefs, positive thoughts, and habit tracking data
  • Your alignment score and emotional streak
  • Todos and goal steps you create

How we use your data

  • To personalise your experience - reframes, affirmations and micro-tasks are based on your journal entries and identity beliefs
  • To track your progress through alignment scores, streaks and analytics
  • To run our ML emotion and behaviour detection on your journal text
  • We do not share your data with any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not sell your data to anyone

How we protect your data

  • Your password is hashed using industry-standard Werkzeug hashing before storage
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption in production
  • Row-Level Security on the database means you can only ever access your own data
  • CSRF protection on every form prevents forged requests
  • Content Security Policy headers block injected scripts
  • Input sanitisation strips dangerous content from all text you submit

Your rights

  • Right to access: You can see all your data through the app at any time
  • Right to erasure: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data using the button below
  • Right to rectification: You can edit your journal entries and update your habits, goals and beliefs at any time

Delete Account

This will permanently delete your account and all your data. This includes your journal entries, intentions, identity beliefs, positive thoughts, habits, todos, goal steps, alignment score and emotional streak. This cannot be undone.

To confirm, type DELETE in the box below and press the button.
