Break it down

Big intentions become real when you turn them into small steps.
Each step should feel doable in a few minutes. You can always add more later.

Why this works

Large goals trigger avoidance because your brain sees them as threats. Breaking them into tiny pieces removes the threat. Each small step is easy enough that your brain does not resist it.
