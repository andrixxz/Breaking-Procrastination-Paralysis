Rewrite as identity beliefs

Turn each intention into an identity statement using "I am" language.
Example: "Finish my project" → "I am someone who follows through on commitments."

Why this works

When you rewrite an intention as an identity statement, you begin shifting what your subconscious looks for as evidence. Your brain starts noticing proof that the new belief is true.
