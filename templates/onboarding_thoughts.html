Add supportive thoughts

For each identity belief, add a positive thought that supports it.
These will show up as your daily affirmations.

How is this different from identity beliefs?

  • Identity belief = who you are becoming. ("I am someone who...")
  • Positive thought = what you say to yourself on hard days. A kind reminder in your own voice. Say these to yourself when doubt or discouraging thoughts show up.

Examples:

  • Belief: "I am someone who follows through." → Thought: "I do not have to finish everything today. I just have to start."
  • Belief: "I am someone who moves their body." → Thought: "Even five minutes counts. Movement is movement."
  • Belief: "I am someone who makes time for learning." → Thought: "One page is still progress. Small steps add up."
Why this works

Supportive thoughts written in your own words bypass the resistance your brain puts up against generic advice. These are yours, so they feel true.
